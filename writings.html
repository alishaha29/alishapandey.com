Bubble

Lately I realized that sometimes when you let someone in your bubble it's not that bad. You get to stay in a bigger and happier bubble. Is it good to be an open book once in a while? I am scared because people might just lose interest after reading you for a while and the pages remain unturned. It is never about the next chapter it's about the effort and time you invest in that person to turn the pages, the person becomes a part of it too.

People that enter our life happen to be a character in a chapter but why not the whole book? Why does the journey start and never end? And that hurts because you make stories about them and one day they disappear and you're not sure if you are to stop or continue without that person. And it again takes me to the part where I want to be in the bubble all by myself. It kind of sucks when it's your book but someone else is writing the story.

You sometimes don't have a name for few a relationships and maybe that's awesome because you vibe with that person, you feel good talking to them. You've just started talking, never met but you trust them and they trust you too. You can be you and there are no commitments. They unintentionally play an important part in your life and you're not sure if they're meant to stay or one day they disappear.

How does the cloud make you stay.

How soothing is it for the eye
Be it glimpse of you with the moon
or your dark side in the monsoon

how do you wash away all my pain
