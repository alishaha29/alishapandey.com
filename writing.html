Our Happy Place

How would the world be if we'd encourage instead of judge
How would relationships be if we'd talk than hold grudge
Wouldn't it be easy if we'd show some sympathy
How about helping with a bit of empathy
How hard is it to love than to hate
Why do we have to forget memories than recreate
Wouldn't it be easy to compliment than offend
How hard is it to make someone smile and defend
How hard is it to help heal than inflict wounds
Wouldn't it be peaceful when the positivity surrounds
How about a world full of love
How about not bringing people down and help moving above

Living in a Bubble

Lately, I've been living in a bubble
Keeping myself away from trouble
I have been loving this solitude
With the aroma of coffee I brewed
Friends with the sky and the moon
Indulging the smell of rain this monsoon
Not in solitary or gone that far
Just a little more love for the stars
Obsessed with the cool breeze that sway
An addition in my calendar for self love day
I have been being so subtle
Wrapping up my thoughts in a bottle
So happy being in my own bubble
A bubble, keeping me away from trouble

Butterflies

Damn! All those butterflies
All the promises we made were never lies
Growing old together, how the time flies
Cups of coffee and some fries
How we looked at each other in the eyes
Looking back on how we walked all those miles
All the memories that I try to memorize
Damn, it still gives me butterflies
